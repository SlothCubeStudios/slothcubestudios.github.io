A Multiplayer VR Horror Experience

Whispers in the Dark (WIND) is a terrifying multiplayer VR game where players navigate eerie, dimly lit environments while being stalked by the **Cousins**—twisted, otherworldly beings that defy logic.

Key Features:

  • 👁️ **Surreal Horror** – The environment itself shifts and reacts to your presence.
  • 🕵️ **Puzzle-Solving** – Work together to decipher cryptic clues and uncover hidden secrets.
  • 🎧 **Immersive Sound Design** – Footsteps, whispers, and eerie rustling hint at lurking threats.
  • 👥 **Co-op Survival** – Team up with friends to outsmart the ever-adapting **Cousins**.
